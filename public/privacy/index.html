Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 10, 2026

This privacy policy explains how TuDu handles information when you use the app.

Information We Collect

TuDu does not collect, sell, share, or track personal information. The app does not require an account and does not include advertising, analytics, crash reporting, or third-party tracking tools.

Tasks You Create

Task text that you enter in TuDu is used only inside the app. The current app does not send user-created task content to a backend operated by us and does not store it in an account.

Demo Data

TuDu loads sample todo data from JSONPlaceholder at https://jsonplaceholder.typicode.com. This third-party service may receive standard technical information associated with web requests, such as your IP address, browser or device information, and request metadata.

Device Permissions

TuDu does not request access to sensitive device permissions such as location, contacts, camera, microphone, photos, health data, or push notifications.

Children's Privacy

TuDu is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy if the app changes. Any updates will be published on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at kerim@berdimyradov.com.
